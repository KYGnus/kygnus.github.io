KYGnus is dedicated to developing open source solutions that breathe new life into old hardware. Our Linux-based software transforms outdated equipment into powerful storage systems, backup servers, and media centers. We champion transparency in development and believe in making valuable tools accessible to all, particularly those with collective benefits like our medical software offerings.

Through our three product categories - security, services, and AI innovation - we provide sustainable technology solutions that reduce electronic waste while delivering enterprise-level performance. Every product we create upholds the free software philosophy, ensuring users have complete control over their systems.
